🧘‍♀️ Exercise Routine

Building strength isn’t about doing more, it's about doing it consistently. Here's a simple and effective routine:

  • 5-minute warm-up: brisk walking or jumping jacks
  • 15 squats • 10 pushups • 20 lunges (10 each leg)
  • 30 seconds plank • 10 sit-ups
  • Repeat for 3 rounds
  • End with 5 minutes stretching & deep breathing

🥗 Nutrition Notes

Your body is a temple — fuel it with kindness. Add these basics into your daily meals:

  • 🌾 Whole grains like oats, brown rice, or quinoa
  • 🥬 Green veggies: spinach, broccoli, cucumber
  • 🍎 Seasonal fruits for natural energy & fiber
  • 🥚 Lean protein: eggs, dal, paneer or tofu
  • 💧 At least 8 glasses of water — hydration is strength
